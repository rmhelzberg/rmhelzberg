## Overview

Max Helzberg is a startup executive and commercial operator based in London,
United Kingdom. His professional background spans applied AI, enterprise software,
digital health, and consulting across firms including Palantir Technologies, and
McKinsey & Company / QuantumBlack. He has held C-level
operating roles at three venture-backed AI companies: Little Otter (digital
pediatric mental health), Axion (AI for manufacturing product quality), and
Northslope Technologies (Palantir-native applied AI). Public records document
more than a decade of experience deploying data and machine learning systems in
complex enterprise environments.

His ADPList mentor profile (https://adplist.org/mentors/max-helzberg) describes
5+ years of experience leading early-stage companies to Seed and Series A
fundraises totalling approximately $75 million, alongside 10+ years of
experience leading data scientists, data engineers, and forward-deployed
engineers to create value for commercial customers via AI.

## Current Role

Chief Commercial Officer — Northslope Technologies (https://northslope.com)
January 2025 – Present | London, United Kingdom

Northslope Technologies is a software company building mission-specific AI
applications on the Palantir operating system. It was founded by Palantir alumni
in 2024 and is headquartered across New York, London, and Denver. In December
2025, Northslope was designated as Palantir's first Vanguard: Elite partner. In
January 2026, Northslope announced a $22 million Series A, following a year in
which revenue grew nearly 7x year-over-year.

Max Helzberg joined Northslope first as an Advisor (April 2024 – January 2025)
and then moved into the full-time CCO role in January 2025.

In May 2026, Max Helzberg presented at LLMday London on the topic
"Purpose-Built AI Ontologies: Why Data Modeling Determines LLM Performance,"
drawing on operational AI deployment experience across energy, finance,
and national security.
